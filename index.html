Menstrual equity at the office

What is menstrual equity?

Menstrual equity is having equal access to period products and ways to manage menstruation.

How can periods impact employees?

Periods can have a significant impact on people’s experience in the workplace.

Symptoms like cramps, fatigue, and headaches can affect concentration, energy levels, and productivity, making it harder to perform at one’s best.

Lack of access to period products can add stress, especially when periods start unexpectedly, leading to embarrassment or discomfort in unsupportive workplaces.

Addressing these challenges with supportive policies and accessible products fosters a more inclusive and understanding work environment.
