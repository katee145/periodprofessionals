Menstrual equity at the office

What is menstrual equity?

Menstrual equity is having equal access to period products and ways to manage menstruation.

How can periods impact employees?

Periods can have a significant impact on people’s experience in the workplace.

Symptoms like cramps, fatigue, and headaches can affect concentration, energy levels, and productivity, making it harder to perform at one’s best.

For some, the lack of access to period products can add stress, especially if periods start unexpectedly. This can lead to feelings of embarrassment or discomfort, particularly in workplaces that aren’t supportive or equipped with essential resources.

Recognising and addressing these challenges through supportive policies and accessible products helps create a more inclusive, understanding work environment for everyone.
